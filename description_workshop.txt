[h1]INFO[/h1]

Open Source at GitHub. Report issues there or at forum page.

NOTE: "Shop Expander" mod is recommended and may be necessary for shop items to appear.
[h1]Features[/h1]

[list]
[*]"WikiThis" mod support.
[*]Furniture.
[*]Placeable versions of non-placeable vanilla tiles.
[*]Inclinometer: Smooths nearby terrain using your 1st Hammer. Can be reversed to un-smooth terrain.
[*]Nature Globe: Prevents you from destroying plants nearby (similar to Guide of Environmental Preservation.
[/list]
